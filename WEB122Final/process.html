Design Brief

While the previous site fell flat mostly in terms of responsiveness, there are still quite a few departments in which it can be built/immproved upon.

  • The primary goal is establishing actual responsive behavior within the site's well-constructed presentation
    • Condensed/truncated Navigation elements for mobile use
    • Varied levels of scaling, especially for mobile devices
  • There should be a major emphasis on color to both influence the end-user's emotions and guide them to any resources/services applicable to them.
    • Blue to guide families due to its calming effects.
    • Green to guide youths and provide them with a sense of "going in the right direction"
    • Purple for individuals so as to influence them into feeling a bit more confident.
  • Interactables and Images should be large enough for vision-impaired and technology-inexperienced users to understand. and easily access
  • Sloped corners and dulled edges should be applied to as to contribute to a more clean and comforting user experience.
