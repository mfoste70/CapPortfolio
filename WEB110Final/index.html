Recent Endeavors and Achievments

Please don't take these seriously, It's basically just a journal/blog entry.

Out of something between boredom and madness, I have partitioned my laptop's hard drive just so I could install a Linux Distro. I want to try and understand Linux as a whole more, so hopefully this won't just be a waste of time/effort/disk-space

It seems like Ubuntu has fallen from grace, as most sources I found post-2021 recommended Fedora as the "Basic Default" of Linux workstations. I've tinkered with a few in the past, but Fedora (with KDE Plasma) has actually been a great fit for my Windows-trained brain. It's nice having something that I can properly call my own computer and OS without the potentially problematic foundation of Windows 11, but some of its quirks and regulations can get a bit annoying.
